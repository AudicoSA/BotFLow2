South African SMEs spend countless hours on repetitive tasks: responding to customer WhatsApp messages, manually entering receipt data, and searching for information. Most automation tools are priced in USD and don't support local payment methods.

### The Solution
BotFlow is an all-in-one business automation platform built specifically for South African businesses:

**AI Assistant (R499/month)**
- ChatGPT-powered business assistant
- Custom prompts for your business context
- Unlimited conversations
- Knowledge base integration

**WhatsApp Assistant (R499/month)**
- Automated customer responses 24/7
- QR code onboarding - no number transfer needed
- Works with your existing WhatsApp Business
- Smart handoff to human agents

**Receipt Assistant (R99/user/month)**
- AI-powered receipt scanning

- Expense categorization
- CSV/PDF export for accounting

- All three services combined
